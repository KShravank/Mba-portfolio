Appendix B — Summary of Revisions & Academic Integrity Notes

B1. How LLMs Were Used

I used LLMs (ChatGPT) to:

  • brainstorm ideas
  • generate draft outlines
  • produce first-pass text for some sections
  • assist with formatting and structure

LLMs were not used to produce final content.

B2. How I Ensured Accuracy

All final content was:

  • fact-checked
  • rewritten in my own words
  • aligned to my actual work experience (VMware, Urban Ladder, etc.)
  • revised to match MBA-level expectations
  • reviewed for clarity and professionalism

B3. Ethical Use of AI

I followed academic honesty guidelines:

  • No copy-pasting of unedited LLM text
  • All LLM drafts are disclosed in this appendix
  • All final text is original and written by me
  • I corrected inaccuracies and removed generic statements

B4. Reflection on LLM Limitations

Through this project, I learned that LLMs:

  • generate helpful structure, but lack contextual precision
  • can produce generic or inaccurate descriptions of my experience
  • require human judgment for accuracy, tone, and meaning
  • work best as brainstorming tools, not content creators

This aligns with course teachings on responsible AI use.
